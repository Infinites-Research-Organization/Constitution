# Constitution of the Infinites Research Organization

This document outlines our foundational governance framework and core research principles.

## Issues Pending Upcoming Vote

* **Mitigation of External Influence:** Protocols to prevent government, regulatory, or corporate funding from compromising our research neutrality.
* **Technological Boundaries:** Absolute limits on what we will not develop, such as mass-surveillance systems or offensive cyber-weapons.
* **Publication Architecture:** Deciding between an unconditional "Open Source by Default" model or a case-by-case framework

---

## Research Focus & Goals

Our mission is to build, audit, and open-source technologies that protect individual privacy, maintain digital sovereignty, and enhance self-defense.

### 1. Software & Engineering

* **Security Auditing & Penetration Testing:** Open utilities for network defense, security auditing, and vulnerability assessment.
* **Signals Intelligence & Detection:** Frameworks to detect rogue or unwanted surveillance devices, such as hidden Wi-Fi cameras and tracking tags.
* **Advanced R&D:** Researching and Developing machine learning architectures, cryptographic protocols, and secure wireless communication/detection
* **Passive RF Detection & Signal Processing:** Developing algorithms for passive radar and RF-based object detection utilizing commercial off-the-shelf Software Defined Radio (SDR) equipment.

### 2. Anti-Surveillance Countermeasures

* **Algorithmic Defense & Data Poisoning:** Development of adversarial machine learning frameworks, data-poisoning tools, and privacy-preserving data perturbation techniques designed to prevent mass-scraping and unauthorized model training on personal data.
* **Biometric Disruption:** Designing AI-camouflage and physical patterns to disrupt unauthorized biometric, facial, and object recognition systems.
* **Signal & Hardware Mitigation:** Material science research into radio-wave blocking, RF-absorbing materials, physical shielding, and acoustic countermeasures.

### 3. Unmanned Aerial System (UAS) Defense

* **Drone Detection:** Engineering multi-modal detection systems leveraging RF scanning, computer vision, and acoustic analysis to identify unauthorized low-altitude aerial vehicles.
* **Defensive Mitigation:** Researching non-destructive, passive, and legal mitigation strategies—such as directional RF shielding, localized protocol analysis, and early-warning telemetry verification—to safeguard private airspace against surveillance drones.

## Platform & Legal Compliance

* **Dual-Use Content:** All software and research are developed strictly for educational, defensive, and privacy-preserving purposes.
* **GitHub Policy Alignment:** In compliance with GitHub's Acceptable Use and Active Malware or Exploits Policies, this organization supports legitimate dual-use security research and privacy tool development. The platform will never be used as attack infrastructure, for malicious exploit distribution, or for generating active network disruptions. All code is intended to empower individuals to audit their own environments and secure their personal data.
